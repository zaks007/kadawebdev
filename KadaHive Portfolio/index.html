Welcome to KadaHive

Kadahive is an innovation and technology Hub. It was founded in 2021, during Mal. Nasiru El rufai, the former governor of kaduna state. The hub aim at driving innovations and makeing impact to young poople in the community.

Our Services

Tech Tranings

We offer a wide range of tech training programs designed to equip individuals with the skills needed to thrive in the digital age. From coding bootcamps to data science workshops, our training sessions are tailored to meet the needs of beginners and advanced learners alike.

Startup Incubation

Our startup incubation program provides aspiring entrepreneurs with the resources, mentorship, and support needed to turn their ideas into successful businesses. We offer access to funding, networking opportunities, and expert guidance to help startups thrive.

Community Engagement

At KadaHive, we believe in the power of community. We organize events, workshops, and hackathons to foster collaboration and innovation among tech enthusiasts. Join us to connect with like-minded individuals and contribute to the growth of the tech ecosystem.

Our Projects

Project 1: Tech for Good

This project focuses on leveraging technology to address social issues. We collaborate with NGOs and community organizations to develop tech solutions that make a positive impact in areas such as education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability.

Project 2: Innovation Hub

Our Innovation Hub serves as a space for creative minds to come together and brainstorm new ideas. We provide access to resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities to help innovators turn their concepts into reality.

Project 3: Digital Literacy Campaign

We are committed to promoting digital literacy in our community. Through workshops and training sessions, we aim to empower individuals with the skills needed to navigate the digital world confidently.
